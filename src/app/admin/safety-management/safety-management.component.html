Incident Description

Worker was performing routine maintenance on the hydraulic pump of Haul Truck #42. While loosening a pressure line, a small residual pressure spray caused the worker's hand to slip, resulting in a minor abrasion against the metal housing. First aid was administered on-site. No loss of time occurred.

Action Taken

Administered first aid on-site. Worker returned to duty after 30 minutes.

Preventive Measures

Update depressurization procedure checklist for this specific hydraulic model.
